Rabblefish plays a vibrant mix of jazz, funk and blues, ranging from progressive to old school. The music features original compositions, with a heavy emphasis on groove. Guitarist, composer and Purple Squirrel co-founder Chris Malley leads the band, which features a rotating lineup of Colorado's finest musicians.

www.rabblefishmusic.com

Purple Squirrel co-founder Bob Schlesinger is a pianist, producer and composer whose work covers diverse styles, including jazz, blues, R&B and classical. Bob leads the Bob Schlesinger Trio.
